# Logo Setup Instructions

Please save your VenueConnect logo image as:

**File path:** `public/logo.png`

The logo should be:
- PNG format with transparent background
- The pink/magenta "VenueConnect" logo from your design
- Recommended dimensions: 400x120 pixels (or similar aspect ratio)

Once you add the logo file, it will automatically appear in:
- Header/Navbar (top of every page)
- Footer (bottom of every page)

The footer version will be inverted to white to show on the dark background.
